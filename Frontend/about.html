Our Story

Making "Unlimited" Actually Mean Unlimited

DropZone Networks was born from a simple observation: Kenyan homes and businesses were paying premium prices for capped, throttled, and unreliable internet. We believed our communities deserved better.

Starting in Nairobi, our vision was to build an infrastructure that doesn't just connect people, but empowers them. We set out to redefine broadband by removing data caps, eliminating artificial throttling, and ensuring that whether you are a remote worker, a small business owner, or a family streaming movies, your connection remains steadfast.

Today, we are bridging the digital divide with high-tech solutions, proving that world-class, futuristic connectivity can be affordable and accessible right here at home.

The Technology

Next-Generation Infrastructure

At the core of DropZone Networks is a sophisticated blend of Fiber-to-the-Home (FTTH) and advanced wireless broadband technology.

Our fiber-optic backbone operates at the speed of light, providing incredible bandwidth and ultra-low latency. For areas where fiber hasn't reached yet, our enterprise-grade wireless network beams high-speed connectivity directly to your location. This dual-approach ensures that our network grid is robust, redundant, and ready for the future of digital innovation.
